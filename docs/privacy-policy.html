Privacy Policy

App: Arandouria
Developer: Andreas Schulz
Last updated: June 2026

Summary

Arandouria does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All game progress is stored locally on your device only.

Data We Store

  • Game progress (current dungeon run, character stats, inventory) — stored locally in your device's app container via standard iOS file storage. Never leaves your device.
  • Meta-progression (permanent unlocks, meta-level) — stored locally via UserDefaults. Never leaves your device.
  • Settings (control mode, rendering mode, audio volumes) — stored locally via UserDefaults. Never leaves your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account or sign-in required
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, camera, or microphone access
  • No data is shared with third parties for advertising or marketing

In-App Purchases

Arandouria offers optional in-app purchases (character classes, dungeon expansions) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not handle payment data directly. Apple's privacy policy applies to all purchase transactions.

Crash Reporting

Arandouria uses Sentry, a crash- and error-monitoring service (data processed in the EU, Frankfurt region), to help us find and fix bugs. When the app crashes or encounters an unexpected error, a diagnostic report is sent automatically. This report contains only: device model, OS version, app version, and the program's stack trace at the point of the error. It does not contain your name, email address, IP address, game progress, or any other personally identifiable information.

Children

Arandouria does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains no age-gated content or features requiring parental consent.
